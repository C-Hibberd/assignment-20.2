About this website

It's purpose and future

I created this website witht the idea of hosting a significant chunk of recipies from the several cooking books i have written online, in order to make them more readily available to people. Whilst i may update it in the future, it is not a primary project and so may stay exactly the same for quite a while

Whilst a significant amount of my recipies will be hosted on this site, it will not be every single one I have written about in one of my books, and so if you would like a more complete collection, please ring me at +44 07821 424366 to discuss purchase of any one of my cooking books.
